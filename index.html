Hello, welcome to Contextum!

Contextum lets you analyze a text and extract the phrases in which words appear.

1To start, enter one or more words you wold like to find in a text that can be a book, a song or an article.

2Then, upload the text as a .txt file.

3Finally, click on the start button to see the context in which the words you chose are used. If the search gives no results, try with some other words.
