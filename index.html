Hello, welcome to Contextum!

You can use the blue arrows in the upper-left corner to navigate through this short introduction.

Contextum lets you analyze a text and extract the phrases in which words appear.

To start, enter one or more words you would like to find in a text, such as an ebook or an article.

In the Words section you can find the list of words that have been entered. You can delete a word by clicking on it.

After you have entered the words, upload the text file in .txt format.

Finally, click on the start button to see the context in which the words you chose are used. If the search gives no results, try with some other words.
